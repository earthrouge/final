Our goal as a company is to discover and promote new, young, and most importantly local talent. Bananaphilia started as a dream of two college guys unsatisfied with the music attributed to their country. In their journey to create they soon discovered they were alone in this battle. With nothing more than their wits and a bank loan Ymir Lopez y Fernando German set to build their own record company to give young artists the opportunity that they never had, and in turn give their country what they've always wanted. Something to be proud of.

With over 6 different artists published, over 10 years of experience and dozens of awards Bananaphilia Records has been a massive force pushing for a better music scene in the D.R. with no intention of stopping. Our search for new talent has taken us from the coast and into the mountains to give us the best this country has to offer, who knows maybe you've got it too.
